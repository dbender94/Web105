Why visit Disney World?

You shout… “I’M GOING TO WALT DISNEY WORLD!!!!” YES! It’s time to celebrate! You are going to a better place. This is going to be Fun!

With great new attractions, experiences and places to eat on the way, 2020 is shaping up to be an absolutely thrilling year for guests to visit Walt Disney World, The Most Magical Place on Earth.

MICKEY'S AND MINNIES RUNAWAY RAILWAY

Mickey and Minnie’s Runaway Railway: Believe it or not, Mickey Mouse has never starred in his own ride at a Disney theme park — but that all changes on March 4, 2020, with the premiere of this attraction at Disney’s Hollywood Studios! Using cutting-edge technologies to transform classic 2-D cartoons into immersive 3-D adventures, it’s your ticket to entering the worlds of Mickey and Minnie’s beloved animated films. Mickey and minni's runaway railaway is every little kids dream come true! The whole family is guarnteed to gain a fun, laughing, loveable memory onboard this train!!

TOWER OF TERROR

Enter the rickety, elevator-style lift, strap yourself in and prepare to discover what lies beyond the darkest corner of your imagination. Shriek in terror as you’re suddenly propelled up and down the abandoned shaft—unexpectedly dropping and rising—as you hear the sound of cables snapping and metal clanging overhead. Will you make it back to the real world… or will you become a permanent resident of the Twilight Zone? This attraction includes sudden, fast drops in a dark, enclosed space which may be frightening to some Guests.

STITCH'S GREAT ESCAPE

Amid a row of blinking monitors, begin your intergalactic training session and discover what it means to be a guard with the Galactic Federation Prisoner Teleport Center. Suddenly, a mysterious Level 3 prisoner is caught—and it’s headed your way! Report to the High Security chamber to assist with the incoming teleportation of this dangerous prisoner. The High-Security Prisoner Transport Chamber Take a seat around a floor-to-ceiling tube. As the teleportation begins, the prisoner is revealed to be none other than free-spirited Stitch. He naturally manages to break out of the transport chamber. Beware as he wreaks hilarious havoc among the audience members—in the dark!
